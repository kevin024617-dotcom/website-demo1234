About Chetan Earth Movers

Our Story

25+ Years of Trusted Service

Chetan Earth Movers was established in 1999 with a vision to provide reliable and efficient earthmoving services in Bangalore and Karnataka. What started as a small operation has grown into one of the region's most trusted names in excavation and site development.

Over the past 25+ years, we have successfully completed over 30,000 projects ranging from small residential excavations to large-scale highway infrastructure works. Our journey reflects our commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we operate with a skilled workforce of 60+ trained professionals and a modern fleet of equipment, serving major infrastructure developers, builders, and contractors.
